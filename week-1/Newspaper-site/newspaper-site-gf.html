Tapie wins Adidas case

Sept. 30, 2005

A French court on friday ordered the state to pay 135 million to the former maverick business mogul Bernard Tapie over issues in the sale of Adidas Sports Goods 10 years ago.

Tapie, 62, originally demanded a much larger sum of 990 million on the grounds that he was defrauded in the sale to Robert-Louis Dreyface.
The sale that took place in 1993 and 1994 was a pivotal event in the companies history.
